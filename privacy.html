Privacy Policy for AyahVerse

AyahVerse is a Quran study application designed to help users read, listen, and study the Quran with tafsir and hadith references.

Information Collection

AyahVerse does not collect, store, or share personal user data.

External Data Sources

The application may connect to external APIs to retrieve Quran text, tafsir, translations, and hadith data.

Local Storage

Some content may be cached locally on the user's device to improve performance.

Third-Party Services

The app may access publicly available APIs for Quranic and Hadith content.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if new features are added.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy please contact the developer.
